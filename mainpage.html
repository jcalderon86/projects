East River POLICY MANUAL

Preface

This policy manual reflects a commitment to maintain the highest safety and quality standards. This is essential to our primary goal of operating East River safely, reliably and efficiently, while protecting the health and safety of station personnel and the general public.
It is expected that these policies will be observed and administered in a manner, which is consistent with both their essence and their intent. Employees and contractor staff are expected to consistently apply these policies.

East River CODE OF ETHICS

As TechCorp East River employees, we are expected to:

  • Adhere to all applicable codes, standards, regulations, policies and procedures and never be party to their evasion.
  • Fulfill responsibilities in the best interest of East River.
  • Engage in no activities involving the Company which are inconsistent with the conscientious performance of our duties.
  • Approach our work with efficiency, economy and earnest effort, without compromising safety or quality.
  • Correct mistakes and prevent their recurrence.
  • Never attempt to conceal errors.
  • Accept criticism with an open mind and constructive attitude.
  • Accept responsibility for our actions Demonstrate a commitment to excellence by working toward exceptional performance.
  • Uphold our values, always being conscious of safety.
  • Always treat others with respect and dignity.
